Stack Deck is a platform that helps developers find companies based on their tech stack.



Features

StackDeck offers a developer friendly way for finding tech companies.

Informed job hunt

With StackDeck you can browse companies based on their tech stack, analyze popularity of different tools and find companies with stack matching your knowledge.

Open API

Not interested in GUI? Search companies with our blazing fast API. Analise the popularity of different frameworks and find the best match for you with a single request.

Growing Database

New companies are scraped and added continuously. The api is free and open for everyone. We strongly encourage creation of new projects that utilize it.

Free Forever

StackDev is free for developers and will always be. There are no monetary incentives behind the project, just shitload of frustration with LinkedIn and other job search services.
